Dogmatic™

Not just automatic, Dogmatic

Taking care of a pet can be a really difficult and time-consuming task. Worrying about whether or not your pet has eaten can cause unnecessary stress.

With DogMatic, you no longer have any worries. We’ll notify you when your dog needs to be fed, and you can rest easy.

Requirements Description
1 Quick Fast and easy to use and understand. That means, have all the necessary buttons accessible.
2 Simple It won´t have a lot of information to overwhelm the user. It will only have essential buttons, and a menu to organize additional information.
3 Attractive It will have uniform colors to look elegant and friendly and also some icons for the user to find things easily.
4 Dog Themed So that it maintains the purpose of the product.
